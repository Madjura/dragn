index.helpers module¶

Helper methods for index_step.

index.helpers.add_related_to(relations, relation2prov)¶

Calculates “related to” relations. :param relations: Relations that were already found. :param relation2prov: The dictionary mapping relations to the provenance. :return: A dictionary containing the “related to” relations.

index.helpers.generate_relation_provenance_weights(sources, relations)¶

Generates the mapping of predicate tuples to provenance with their score therein.

Writes the inverted index to the database. :param index: The index. :return:

index.index_step module¶

index_step of the dragn pipeline. Generates and writes files containing the results of the processing.

index.index_step.generate_relation_values(relations, corpus, alias)¶

Generates the file containing the mapping of SPO-triple to (provenance, score).

Parameters:
  • relations – The existing relations.
  • corpus – The corpus of the memstore created in knowledge_base_compute.
  • alias – The Alias of the texts that were processed.
index.index_step.index_step(alias)¶

Performs the index_step step of the dragn pipeline.

Parameters:alias – The Alias of the texts that were processed.
index.index_step.make_relation_list(relations, alias)¶

Creates a mapping of token: set of relations.

Parameters:
  • relations – The relations calculated in knowledge_base_compute.
  • alias – The Alias of the texts that were processed.
Returns:

A dictionary mapping tokens to their relation partners and scores.
index.index_step.make_relation_weights(relation_dictionary, alias)¶

Writes the shortform of relations between tokens to a file. The format is: token token2score

Parameters:
  • relation_dictionary – A dictionary mapping a token to its relations.
  • alias – The Alias of the texts that were processed.
